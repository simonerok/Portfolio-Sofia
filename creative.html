CREATIVE PROJECTS

In my free time, I draw and constantly work on improving my ability to express myself creatively both digitally and on paper. Additionally, I have a strong desire to tell stories and to create something that is valuable and evokes emotions in others.

Drawings

I am passionate about drawing and expressing my creativity. While attending Rudolf Steiner Skolen in Aarhus for grades 0-6, I had the opportunity to develop my skills in color theory, shapes, and drawing.


GAME DESIGN

During the first semester, we were tasked with developing a game. To begin the process, I started with sketching and drawing. Later, I transferred my designs to Adobe Illustrator and used JavaScript for coding the game. This semester i learned the basics of JavaScript and how to place items in CSS and make animations using CSS. Furthermore i gained experience in using Adobe Illustrator.
